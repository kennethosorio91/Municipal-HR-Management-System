Frequently Asked Questions

How do I file a leave request? +
You can submit your leave request by logging into the HRMS and going to the "Leave Management" section. Be sure to attach the necessary documents if required.
I forgot to clock in/out. What should I do? +
Please contact your supervisor or the HR department to report the issue. They will guide you on the next steps.
Where can I update my contact or personal information? +
You can update your contact or personal information by logging into the HRMS and navigating to the "Profile" section.
How do I access my payslip or salary history? +
You can access your payslip or salary history by logging into the HRMS and going to the "Payroll" section.
